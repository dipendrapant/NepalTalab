## Tax Brackets

The income tax rates in Nepal are progressive. Here are the current tax brackets:

- Up to NPR 400,000: 1%
- NPR 400,001 to 600,000: 10%
- NPR 600,001 to 1,000,000: 20%
- Above NPR 1,000,000: 30%
